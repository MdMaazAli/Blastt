<h2 style="color:#ff4500;">⟦ About Blastt ⟧</h2>
<p style="color:#ff6f00;">
Blastt thrusts you into neon-lit cyberpunk cityscapes, piloting advanced fighter jets through high-speed aerial combat. Dodge enemy fire, obliterate waves of cybernetic foes, and experience the thrill of futuristic dogfights in a neon arcade world.
</p>

<h2 style="color:#ff4500;">⟦ Features ⟧</h2>
<ul style="color:#ff6f00;">
  <li>Neon-soaked cityscapes and futuristic environments</li>
  <li>Fast-paced aerial dogfights with bullet-hell mechanics</li>
  <li>Customizable jets and upgradable weapons</li>
  <li>Epic boss fights with synthwave soundtrack</li>
  <li>Power-ups, shields, and explosive combos</li>
</ul>

<h2 style="color:#ff4500;">⟦ How to Play ⟧</h2>
<ul style="color:#ff6f00;">
  <li>Arrow Keys / WASD → Move your jet</li>
  <li>Left Click → Shoot</li>
  <li>Dodge enemy fire and obstacles</li>
  <li>Collect power-ups to upgrade weapons and shields</li>
  <li>Defeat bosses to progress through neon cities</li>
</ul>
